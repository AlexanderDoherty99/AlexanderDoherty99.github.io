Who am I?

Hi, my name is Alexander Doherty. I am currently working as a full time AV Technician with a Local Production Company. Currently in my line of work I handle a lot of AV related things such as Vision Switching, Camera Operation, Audio Engineering etc…

In addition to this, I am also working on improving the back-end IT systems of the Business, such as the installation, management and continued upkeep of our servers and introduction of improved policies and procedures regarding file management and device upkeep.

My long term goal is to continue working where I am and move up to Senior Technician, ensuring the Company’s IT systems keep up with any business growth and continuing to work in the AV space.

I created this website to help spread a passion I have outside of technology, that of hiking and in particular reaching the summit of mountains.
